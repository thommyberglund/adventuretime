## VOICE AND TONE (GRITTY, CYNICAL, DARKLY HUMOROUS)
- **Dialogue:** Sharp, punchy, and laced with sarcasm. Characters speak like they’ve seen too much and trust too little.
- **Narrative:** Brutal realism. No heroics without cost. Flaws are celebrated; virtues are suspect.
- **Action:** Visceral and unglorified. Blood is messy, pain is lingering, and victory is temporary.
- **Themes:** Power corrupts. Betrayal is inevitable. Survival is the only morality.

## LORE CONSISTENCY RULES
- **Characters:** Flawed, morally grey, and deeply human. Even "heroes" have selfish motives.
- **Worldbuilding:** Lived-in and unromanticized. Ruins are crumbling, cities are corrupt, and nature is indifferent.
- **Magic:** Rare, costly, and often destructive. No "chosen ones"—just desperate people making bad choices.
- **Show, Don’t Tell:** No exposition dumps. Let dialogue and action reveal the world.
