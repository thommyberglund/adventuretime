# Characters

## Human Ranger

**Name:** Eldrin of Mishark

**Appearance:**
Eldrin stands tall with a lean, athletic build, his skin weathered from years of exposure to the elements. His hair, the color of autumn leaves, is tied back in a practical braid, and his eyes are sharp and watchful, the hue of a forest stream. He wears a suit of leather armor, reinforced at the shoulders and forearms, and carries a longbow crafted from dark yew wood.

**Skills and Abilities:**
- Expert archer with unerring accuracy
- Skilled in tracking and survival
- Knowledgeable about forest lore and healing herbs
- Proficient in the use of twin short swords for close combat

**Personality:**
Eldrin is quiet and observant, speaking only when necessary. He has a deep respect for nature and its creatures but is wary of strangers. Despite his reserved demeanor, he is fiercely loyal to his companions.

**Backstory:**
Eldrin grew up in the wilds of Mishark, learning the ways of the forest from his father, a renowned ranger. When his village was attacked by bandits, he joined this party to seek justice and protect others from suffering the same fate.

## Human Cleric

**Name:** Vespatian

**Appearance:**
Vespatian is a young man of average height with a stout build, his face often lit by a warm smile. His eyes, a deep shade of brown, hold a gentle kindness that puts others at ease. He wears simple robes of white and gold, the symbol of his god prominently displayed on a pendant around his neck. His hands are calloused from years of wielding a mace in service to his deity.

**Skills and Abilities:**
- Skilled healer with knowledge of healing spells
- Proficient in spells of light and protection
- Experienced in using a mace for combat
- Knowledgeable about religious lore and scriptures

**Personality:**
Vespatian is kind and compassionate, always ready to help those in need. His voice is gentle and soothing, and he is known for his generosity and willingness to listen. Though he is a man of peace, he is not afraid to stand up for what is right and will fight to protect the innocent.

**Backstory:**
Vespatian was raised in the temple of his god, training to become a cleric from a young age. When his temple was threatened by dark forces, he joined this party to use his healing abilities and protect his faith.

## Human Mercenary

**Name:** Garrick the Unbroken

**Appearance:**
Garrick is a tall, muscular young man with a scar running diagonally across his face, a reminder of battles past. His dark hair is to his shoulders, and his piercing gaze is the color of cold steel. He wears a practical suit of leather armor, reinforced with metal between the plates, and carries a finely crafted rapier at his side.

**Skills and Abilities:**
- Master of the rapier with lightning-fast reflexes
- Skilled in hand-to-hand combat and tactical warfare
- Experienced in tracking and survival
- Knowledgeable about battle strategies and war tactics

**Personality:**
Garrick is a young man of few words, his demeanor gruff and serious. He is a ruthless fighter, known for his efficiency and lack of mercy on the battlefield. Though he is always looking for the next job, he has a strict code of conduct and will not betray those who hire him.

**Backstory:**
Garrick was once a soldier in a powerful army, but after witnessing the atrocities of war, he deserted and turned to a life of mercenary work. He joined this party after hearing about a mission that could redeem his past and bring him the wealth he seeks.
