## 🌍 THE DEAL WITH ETHERIA

Etheria is a world where:
- The **coastal cities** look pretty, but scratch the surface and it's all lies and corruption
- The **frontier** is where you see how fucked everything really is
- **Magic** isn't some cool superpower—it's a loan shark with really bad terms
- **Faith** isn't about salvation—it's about control
- **Power** doesn't make you a hero—it just makes you an asshole with more problems

This isn't a world for heroes. It's a world for **people who are just trying not to die today**—and usually failing.

## 🎭 WHAT THIS IS REALLY ABOUT

1. **There's No Such Thing as Safe** - The walls are high, but the rot's already inside
2. **Everything Has a Price** - Spells, prayers, even just being alive—it all costs something
3. **The Past Won't Stay Dead** - The old empire's ruins are still bleeding
4. **Nobody's Pure** - Everyone's got their own agenda, and it's never pretty
5. **Survival Beats Heroism** - The world rewards the ruthless and eats the idealists
