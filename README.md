## 🌍 THE REALITY OF ETHERIA

Etheria is a world where:
- The **coastal cities** gleam with false promises, but scratch the surface and you'll find nothing but corruption and lies.
- The **frontier** is where the mask slips and you see how doomed everything really is.
- **Magic** isn't a gift—it's a loan shark with a rusted blade and terrible terms.
- **Faith** isn't about salvation—it's about control.
- **Power** doesn't make you a hero—it just makes you a bigger target.

This isn't a world for heroes. It's a world for **people who are just trying not to die today**—and usually failing.

## 🎭 THE CORE PILLARS

1. **Nowhere is Safe** - The walls are high, but the rot is already inside.
2. **Everything Has a Price** - Spells, prayers, even just drawing breath—it all costs something.
3. **The Past is Hungry** - The old empire's ruins aren't just bleeding; they're looking for fresh meat.
4. **Morality is a Luxury** - Everyone has an agenda, and none of them are pretty.
5. **Survival Beats Heroism** - The world rewards the ruthless and buries the idealists.

## 📖 HOW TO SURVIVE WRITING THIS

1. **Read the damn files.** Start with `WORLD.md`, then `SETTLEMENTS.md`.
2. **Follow the style guide.** `CONVENTIONS.md` isn't a suggestion; it's the law.
3. **Keep it grounded.** No happy endings. No easy choices. No unearned victories.
4. **Remember the cost.** Every decision leaves a scar.
